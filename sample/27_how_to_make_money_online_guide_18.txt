how to make money online? — Guide #18

PHASE 1: FOUNDATION – VALIDATE YOUR MONEY-MAKING IDEA

Primary objective: Pick one online income stream that matches your skills and market demand.
Critical action: Run a 7-day validation sprint. Post a free lead magnet (cheat sheet, checklist, or mini-course) on Reddit, Facebook Groups, or niche forums. Use a simple landing page with Carrd or Gumroad. Drive 100 targeted visitors and collect 20 email sign-ups.
Milestone to move forward: 20 sign-ups prove demand. If you hit zero, pivot the offer or audience and repeat the sprint.

PHASE 2: MINIMUM VIABLE OFFER – CREATE A PAY-WALL THAT CONVERTS

Primary objective: Turn the validated lead magnet into a paid product under $50.
Critical action: Build a 30-minute video, 5-page PDF, or one-hour coaching call. Price it at $19–$49. Use Payhip or Ko-fi for instant checkout. Offer a 48-hour flash sale to the 20 sign-ups from Phase 1.
Milestone to move forward: Three sales within 48 hours. If you get zero, tweak the price, guarantee, or sales page and re-run the flash sale.

PHASE 3: TRAFFIC SCALING – DRIVE PAID OR ORGANIC VISITORS DAILY

Primary objective: Generate 100 cold visitors per day to your offer.
Critical action: Choose one channel. For paid: run $5/day TikTok or Facebook ads to a lookalike audience. For organic: post 3 short-form videos daily on TikTok or YouTube Shorts, each with a CTA to your offer.
Milestone to move forward: 30 consecutive days of 100+ daily visitors and 5+ sales per week. If traffic dips below 100, double down on the channel or switch.

PHASE 4: AUTOMATION – REPLACE YOUR TIME WITH SYSTEMS

Primary objective: Reduce hands-on work to 2 hours per day while maintaining revenue.
Critical action: Set up a 3-email welcome sequence in ConvertKit or MailerLite. Automate order delivery with Zapier or Payhip. Outsource customer support to a VA for $5/hour on OnlineJobs.ph.
Milestone to move forward: Revenue stays flat or grows while you work 2 hours daily for 30 days. If revenue drops, audit the automation and fix leaks.

PHASE 5: SCALING – MULTIPLY REVENUE WITHOUT MULTIPLYING WORK

Primary objective: Add a second income stream that leverages the same audience.
Critical action: Create a $97–$197 upsell (course, template pack, or group coaching). Promote it in the welcome sequence and on the thank-you page. Use the same traffic channel from Phase 3.
Milestone to move forward: The upsell generates 20% of total revenue for 30 days. Once hit, repeat Phase 5 with a third product or expand to a new audience.
